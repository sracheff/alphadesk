High-Risk Small Account Growth Mode

Add a special mode called “Small Account 40% Weekly Growth Mode.”

This mode is for starting with $100–$200 and looking for high-risk opportunities that have the potential to grow the account by at least 40% week over week.

The app should clearly label this as speculative / high-risk, not traditional investing.

The app should scan for setups where a 40% weekly return is possible, including:

* short-term calls
* short-term puts
* high-momentum stocks
* earnings volatility plays
* unusual volume
* IV expansion opportunities
* breakout setups
* oversold bounce setups
* small-cap momentum
* sector news catalysts

For every trade idea, the app should show:

* starting account balance
* max amount risked
* estimated upside
* estimated downside
* probability of success
* break-even point
* expected move
* liquidity/spread warning
* expiration date risk
* IV crush risk
* stop-loss suggestion
* profit-taking suggestion
* “why this could move 40%”
* “why this could fail”

The app should not just find cheap contracts.
It should find trades where the math supports the possibility of a 40% gain.

Example logic:

* Account size: $150
* Weekly target: 40%
* Required weekly gain: $60
* Max risk per trade: user-defined, default 25–50% of account
* Target trade return needed: 40%–100% depending on position size

The app should calculate:

Required Return = Target Dollar Gain ÷ Amount Risked

Example:

* If I risk $50 and need to make $60, the trade needs a 120% return.
* If I risk $100 and need to make $60, the trade needs a 60% return.

The app should rank trades by:

* realistic upside
* liquidity
* volatility
* catalyst strength
* technical setup
* risk/reward
* probability of profit

Add a weekly goal tracker:

* Starting Balance
* Weekly Goal
* Current Balance
* Remaining Gain Needed
* Trades Taken
* Win Rate
* Average Gain
* Average Loss
* Risk Remaining

The app should also include warnings like:

This mode is speculative and may result in total loss of capital. It is designed for high-risk opportunity tracking, not guaranteed investing returns.

The app should prioritize opportunities that can realistically create a 40% account increase, but it should always display the chance of losing the full amount risked.
